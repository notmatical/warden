Added sub-agent visibility. A compact agents control in the composer toolbar (next to the worktree toggle) appears whenever a session spawns Task/Agent subagents, showing a live count. It opens a popover overview — each subagent's status (running/done/error), step count, and duration — and selecting one opens a side sheet that replays its prompt, tool activity (with the syntax-highlighted diff/output panels), and final report.
