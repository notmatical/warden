Drag-and-drop attachments on the composer (Claude-Desktop style): drop files, images, or folders onto a session and they appear as a row of thumbnails/chips above the input, removable on hover, with a drop-target highlight while dragging. On send they're referenced in the message so the agent reads them with its tools — image thumbnails render via the asset protocol. Files outside the worktree are staged into the session's attachments dir; the rest are referenced in place.
